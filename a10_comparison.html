Assignment 10 — Deceptive vs. Ethical Comparison

Target Grade: B

This page compares two D3 visualizations built from the same invented dataset on average student sleep hours over eight weeks.

Deceptive Chart

This version uses a truncated y-axis and misleading color framing to exaggerate the increase in sleep hours and make it seem more alarming.

Ethical Chart

This version uses the same dataset, but presents it with a neutral title, consistent color, and a more honest y-axis scale.

Key Difference

  • The deceptive chart makes a small increase look much steeper than it really is.
  • The ethical chart shows that the change is present, but not extreme.
